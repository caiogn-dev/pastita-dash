# Cardapidex Product Roadmap

## Goal

Turn the current dashboard into a SaaS for store owners who need a fast digital menu, order operation, WhatsApp customer workflow and lightweight CRM.

## Phase 1 — Stabilize The SaaS Core

- Make Cardapidex the only visible platform brand.
- Keep all tenant-specific branding controlled by store settings.
- Validate production flows: login, store selection, products, combos, storefront theme, orders, WhatsApp chat and delivery settings.
- Remove store-specific pages from the default product surface.
- Ensure `development` and `feature/cardapidex-brand` stay aligned until the branch strategy is simplified.

## Phase 2 — Owner Onboarding

- Self-service store creation.
- Store slug/domain setup.
- Logo, colors, template and public menu preview.
- Guided first products/combos setup.
- WhatsApp connection checklist.
- Delivery/payment setup checklist.

## Phase 3 — Billing And Plans

Candidate plan structure:

- Starter: public menu, products, basic orders.
- Pro: WhatsApp chat, CRM, coupons, delivery zones.
- Premium: automation, marketing, advanced reports, custom domain.

Required technical pieces:

- Subscription model in backend.
- Billing status on authenticated user/account/store.
- Trial period support.
- Feature gates in frontend and backend.
- Admin override for support.

## Phase 4 — Sales Positioning

Primary promise:

Cardapidex helps a local store sell online without depending only on marketplaces, with menu, orders, WhatsApp and customer history in one place.

Good first niches:

- Snack shops and party kits.
- Bakeries and cake shops.
- Small restaurants and lunchbox stores.
- Açaí, pizza and burger operations.
